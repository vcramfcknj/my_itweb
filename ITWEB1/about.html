Ilocos Sur Polytechnic State College

Ilocos Sur Polytechnic State College is a comprehensive six-campus institution of higher learning mandated to give professional and technical training both in the undergraduate and graduate levels in the fields of economics, agriculture, fishery, trade, home industry, engineering, education, forest research and conservation, management, finance, accounting and business administration, public administration and other fields as may be relevant, besides providing for the promotion of scientific and technological researches which the College deems necessary in carrying out its objectives. The main campus is situated in Santa Maria, Ilocos Sur and the five other campuses are strategically located in Candon City and the municipalities of Narvacan, Santiago, Tagudin, and Cervantes, all in the second district of Ilocos Sur.

About Bachelor of Science in Information Technology

The Bachelor of Science in Information Technology (BSIT) program at Ilocos Sur Polytechnic State College (ISPSC) is designed to equip students with the necessary skills and knowledge to thrive in the rapidly evolving field of Information Technology. The program emphasizes both theoretical foundations and practical applications, ensuring that graduates are well-prepared for various roles within the IT industry.


Students will engage in hands-on learning experiences, participate in industry-related projects, and have opportunities for internships with leading companies. Our dedicated faculty, combined with modern facilities, create an ideal environment for aspiring IT professionals.

Mission and Vision of ITSO

Mission: To provide quality education that fosters student engagement, encourages critical thinking, and prepares graduates for a competitive global workforce.


Vision: To be a leading institution in Information Technology education, producing competent, innovative, and socially responsible graduates.
